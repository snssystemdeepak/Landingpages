The 2026 Cadillac CT5 Cherry Hill, NJ, is a midsize luxury sedan that delivers a strong first impression and an even stronger drive. Now available at Cadillac of Turnersville, this performance-focused sedan offers an elevated experience with advanced technology, bold styling and refined comfort across four trims: Premium Luxury, Sport and two V-Series models. Whether you're looking for athleticism or sophistication, the 2026 CT5 lineup brings both.

Which Cadillac dealers have the 2026 Cadillac CT5 near Cherry Hill, NJ, for test drives?

We do! You can explore all trims of the 2026 Cadillac CT5 Cherry Hill, NJ, right here at Cadillac of Turnersville. Standard configurations at our Cadillac dealership Cherry Hill, NJ, come with a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine generating 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Paired with rear-wheel drive and a 10-speed automatic transmission, this setup delivers a smooth, balanced ride for daily commutes and open-road driving alike. For those craving more power, optional upgrades include a 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 that increases output to 335 horsepower. Step into the CT5-V trim level to enjoy 360 horsepower and 405 lb-ft of torque, enhanced with Magnetic Ride Control and a limited-slip rear differential for sharper handling. At the top of the lineup, the Cadillac CT5-V Blackwing for this model year offers a 668-horsepower supercharged 6.2-liter V8, with your choice of a 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic transmission.

Every 2026 Cadillac CT5 Cherry Hill, NJ, at our Cadillac dealership boasts striking exterior design, starting with sleek LED lighting and sculpted body lines. The Premium Luxury trim rides on 18-inch wheels, while the Sport and V-Series trims feature aggressive styling paired with 19-inch alloys. The CT5-V Blackwing takes things further with a carbon-fiber aero package, quad exhaust tips and ambient exterior lighting. Distinct grille treatments and illuminated door handles add unique touches across Sport and V-Series trims, blending performance with presence.

Inside the 2026 Cadillac CT5 Cherry Hill, NJ, you’ll find seating for five with standard heated front seats and available upgrades to enhance every mile. Mid to upper trims offer leather seating surfaces, while the V-Series introduces carbon-fiber trim and a heated steering wheel. Premium packages add features like ventilated and massaging front seats, a panoramic sunroof and semi-aniline leather upholstery.

Technology in the 2026 Cadillac CT5 Cherry Hill, NJ, centers around a 33-inch advanced LED display with built-in Google™. Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ come standard, with available Super Cruise® on select trims for hands-free driving on compatible highways. The V-Series offers even more with a performance data recorder, head-up display, hands-free decklid and rear camera mirror.

Safety features in the 2026 Cadillac CT5 Cherry Hill, NJ, come standard through the Cadillac Smart System. These include forward collision alerts, lane keep assist, lane change alerts with side blind zone alerts and automatic emergency braking. An HD Surround Vision camera and intersection automatic emergency braking further enhance awareness on crowded roads. Optional safety features on V-Series configurations include blind zone steering assist and rear pedestrian alert, while Super Cruise® adds semi-autonomous convenience for highway driving.
