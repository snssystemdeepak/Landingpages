Meet the 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, from Cadillac of Turnersville

Luxury performance takes a bold turn with the arrival of the 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, now available at Cadillac of Turnersville. This sleek compact sedan delivers an engaging drive, premium interior features and cutting-edge technology across five trims: Luxury, Premium Luxury, Sport, CT4-V and CT4-V Blackwing. Whether you prefer understated elegance or track-ready performance, the 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, offers a tailored experience for every driver.

Which Cadillac dealers have the 2026 Cadillac CT4 near Cherry Hill, NJ, for test drives?

You can find all trims of the 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, right here at Cadillac of Turnersville. Take a test drive to see the power of the standard 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine producing 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive. For added muscle, you can opt for a 2.7-liter Dual Volute turbocharged engine that produces either 310 or 325 horsepower, depending on the configuration. Available all-wheel drive and a 10-speed automatic transmission offer enhanced control and response. At the top of the lineup, the CT4-V Blackwing boasts a 3.6-liter twin-turbo V6 engine rated at 472 horsepower with your choice of a 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic.

The 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, blends athletic design with luxury details. Standard exterior features include 17-inch alloy wheels, body-colored heated mirrors and LED headlamps. Premium trims at our Cadillac dealership offer available 19-inch wheels, a power sunroof and LED front cornering lamps. You’ll also find eye-catching elements like a body-colored rear spoiler, black or bright window moldings and a distinctive grille design depending on the trim. Step inside the 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, to discover standard Inteluxe seating, heated front seats, a heated steering wheel and 12-way power-adjustable driver seats. The cabin seats five and features a split-folding rear seat to enhance cargo space. Optional upgrades include leather seating, ventilated front seats, ambient lighting and alloy sport pedals for added flair.

All trims of the 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, come loaded with advanced tech. Standard features include an 8-inch HD touchscreen with Natural Voice Recognition, wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, an 8-speaker premium audio system and a wireless phone charger. A dual digital Driver Information Center and Wi-Fi® hotspot capability further enhance the connected experience. Available options include a 12-inch reconfigurable digital gauge cluster, integrated navigation, Bose® 14-speaker premium surround sound and a head-up display. The 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, also offers performance-inspired tools like an Integrated Performance Data Recorder and a Surround Vision Recorder. Drivers can opt for SUPER CRUISE™ hands-free assistance on compatible roads.

The 2026 Cadillac CT4 Cherry Hill, NJ, includes a full suite of safety features to give drivers and passengers added confidence. Standard systems include Lane Keep Assist, Forward Collision Alerts, Side Blind Zone Alerts and Rear Cross Traffic Alerts. You’ll also benefit from Lane Departure Warnings and Lane Change Alerts for added highway awareness. Available upgrades include Adaptive Cruise Control, Enhanced Automatic Emergency Braking, Reverse Automatic Braking, a Following Distance Indicator and HD Surround Vision.
