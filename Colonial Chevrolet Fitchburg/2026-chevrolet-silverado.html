Get to Know the 2026 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Leominster, MA, at Colonial Chevrolet

The 2026 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Leominster, MA, is built to deliver the power, durability and comfort today’s truck owners demand. This full-size pickup truck at Colonial Chevrolet offers versatile performance, advanced technology and a comfortable interior, making it equally suited for demanding workdays or relaxing weekend getaways.

Which Chevy dealers have the 2026 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 in Leominster, MA?

Colonial Chevrolet proudly offers the 2026 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Leominster, MA, for test drives. This Chevy truck’s bold exterior starts with functional features like standard LED reflector headlamps, CornerStep rear bumpers for easy bed access and 12 cargo tie-downs to secure loads. The rugged look is completed with black bumpers and 17-inch Ultra Silver painted steel wheels, while available upgrades like 22-inch 6-spoke Low Gloss Black wheels with machined accents, power-retractable assist steps, a Multi-Flex Tailgate and perimeter lighting enhance style and usability.

Every 2026 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Leominster, MA, comes equipped with standard safety technologies such as Automatic Emergency Braking, IntelliBeam automatic high beams, Forward Collision Alerts, Front Pedestrian Braking, Lane Keep Assist and Lane Departure Warnings. Available features like HD Surround Vision, Rear Cross Traffic Braking, Lane Change Alerts with Side Blind Zone Alert and Rear Pedestrian Alert provide additional awareness and protection on the road.

Inside, the 2026 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Leominster, MA, provides a cabin designed for both productivity and comfort with durable vinyl seating, a 40/20/40 split-bench front seat with under-seat storage, rubberized vinyl floor covering and single-zone climate control. For a more premium feel, available options include perforated leather seating, heated and ventilated front seats, heated rear seats, a heated steering wheel and dual-zone automatic climate control. In the 2026 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Leominster, MA, the standard Chevrolet Infotainment 3 System features a 7-inch color touchscreen or an available 13.4-inch touchscreen, wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, Bluetooth®, USB ports, a 6-speaker audio system and Wi-Fi hotspot capability. Available upgrades include a 12.3-inch digital driver display, wireless device charging, Super Cruise hands-free driving, a Head-Up Display and a Bose® Premium Sound System for enhanced audio.

The 2026 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Leominster, MA, offers four engine options to fit a wide range of needs. The standard TurboMax™ engine generates 310 horsepower and 430 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission for dependable performance. For more muscle, drivers can choose the 5.3-liter EcoTec3 V8 with 355 horsepower or the 6.2-liter EcoTec3 V8 producing 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, both paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. For those prioritizing efficiency and torque, the 3.0-liter Duramax® Turbo Diesel offers 305 horsepower and 495 lb-ft of torque, making it ideal for towing and long-distance driving.
