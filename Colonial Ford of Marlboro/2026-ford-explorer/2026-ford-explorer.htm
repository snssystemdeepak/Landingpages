How BlueCruise Works

BlueCruise is an available hands-free driver assistance feature designed to help make driving easier, more enjoyable, and less stressful. With the new Automatic Lane Change feature, you can drive hands-free with fewer interruptions.

Blue Zones

When you enter a pre-qualified section of a divided highway - a Blue Zone - you'll see a prompt on the instrument cluster letting you know BlueCruise is available and ready to assist.*

When you enter a pre-qualified section of a divided highway - a Blue Zone - you'll see a prompt on the instrument cluster letting you know BlueCruise is available and ready to assist.*

Let BlueCruise Assist

Check your Lane-Keeping System is on and tap the Adaptive Cruise control button on your steering wheel and let BlueCruise assist. While you keep your eyes on the road, the system helps handle steering, keeps you centered in your lane, and adjusts your speed as needed - all without you touching the wheel.*

Automatic Lane Change

BlueCruise can detect when there is slower traffic ahead of you, initiate a lane change, accelerate to pass, then move your Explorer back into the original lane ahead of the slower vehicle - all without you putting your hands on the wheel.*

Driver-Facing Camera

To help ensure your eyes stay on the road while your hands are off the wheel, a driver-facing camera tracks eye gaze and head position. This driver-monitoring system adds an additional level of confirmation that you're engaged and ready to resume control at any moment.*

360° Perception

External sensors monitor the environment around your Explorer to help keep you centered in your lane at a set distance from other vehicles.*

Connected Maps

Smart mapping software uses your geolocation to determine if you are in a hands-free Blue Zone and can drive with your hands off the wheel. These maps are updated over the air to ensure you never miss a chance to use BlueCruise.*
