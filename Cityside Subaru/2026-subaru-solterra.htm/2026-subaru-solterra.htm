DriverFocus® DISTRACTION MITIGATION SYSTEM AND FRONT CROSS-TRAFFIC ALERT

Working in conjunction with EyeSight®, the standard DriverFocus® Distraction Mitigation System* is like having an attentive co-pilot along for the ride, available to give a helpful alert if a driver becomes distracted or drowsy. This system is designed to enhance the effectiveness of other safety systems while also increasing passenger safety within the vehicle. If its steering-column-mounted camera detects a distracted, drowsy, or incapacitated driver, it will provide an alert. DriverFocus® also combines with other safety systems such as Emergency Driving Stop System by allowing them to intervene earlier when necessary. Front Cross-Traffic Alert* watches for unexpected traffic from either side and can alert you to approaching vehicles — including those that may not be visible from the driver’s seat.

All-Around Awareness Technologies

The 2026 Solterra features a comprehensive suite of active EV safety features to help protect you on every drive. The Blind-Spot Monitor* system warns you with a visual indicator on each side mirror if it senses a vehicle in your blind spots. Front Cross-Traffic Alert can alert you if unexpected vehicles — even vehicles you may not be able to see — approach from either side of your Solterra. Lane Change Assist* watches for other vehicles as you move into a new lane, helps with steering, and automatically cancels your turn signal. Rear Cross-Traffic Alert* helps warn you of traffic approaching from the side as you back up.
