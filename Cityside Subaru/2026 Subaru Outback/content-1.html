Standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive

Standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive

Capability

Every 2026 Subaru Outback model features the capability of standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive. This system is designed to optimize traction in rain, snow, on bad roads, or even when there’s no road at all. It constantly evaluates acceleration, braking, and cornering in real time to continuously deliver power to all four wheels. If a wheel slips, power is instantaneously transferred to the wheels that have the most grip.

Stability and Control

With its exceptionally balanced layout, Subaru Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive delivers a solid connection with the road and an engaged, responsive drive. The entire system lies along the centerline of the vehicle, balancing weight distribution between the two sides to help provide optimal performance and control.

Fuel Efficiency and Durability

Each 2026 Outback model delivers competitive gas mileage and driving range. Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive has demonstrated its durability and low maintenance over four decades of owner-tested Subaru vehicles so you can be confident your Outback will keep taking you farther year after year.

Up to 9.5 Inches of Ground Clearance and X-MODE®

With up to 9.5 inches of ground clearance and standard X-MODE®, Outback AWD midsize SUVs can take you almost anywhere. X-MODE® works by optimizing the Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive system for maximum traction on slippery surfaces and Hill Descent Control gives you extra stability and confidence when descending more difficult grades. Dual-function X-MODE® allows even more control with settings for snow, dirt, deep snow, and mud.

Upgraded Standard EyeSight® Driver Assist Technology

Upgraded Standard EyeSight® Driver Assist Technology

Subaru EyeSight®

Every Outback SUV features upgraded standard EyeSight® Driver Assist Technology for 2026. EyeSight® acts like an extra set of eyes on the road and if need be, an extra foot on the brake*. EyeSight® includes a number of advanced active safety systems to protect you and your family on every drive. Newly available Hands-Free Assist* can help reduce driver fatigue with automatic steering control under certain conditions. Emergency Stop Assist with Safe Lane Selection*, also newly available, can automatically steer toward the shoulder, bring the vehicle to a stop, and activate the hazard lights in the event the driver is incapacitated. Automatic Emergency Steering can help you avoid a collision by providing automatic steering and pre-collision braking support if it determines there’s enough space to intervene*. EyeSight® and other safety features in the Outback Wilderness have been calibrated to match its increased ground clearance and trail-ready upgrades.

Pre-Collision Braking

Automatic Pre-Collision Braking* helps avoid or reduce frontal impacts by alerting you and applying full braking force in emergency situations. Pre-Collision Throttle Management can reduce the power from the engine to help minimize the possible impact force and frontal impact damage.

Adaptive Cruise Control

Advanced Adaptive Cruise Control with Lane Centering* adds extra convenience and safety on every trip. Just choose the distance you want to keep from the car in front of you and it can take some of the stress out of everyday driving by helping with steering, braking, and throttle control while maintaining a safe distance ahead.

TomTom® Navigation

The MySubaru® Connected Services touchscreen navigation system’s intuitive interface and high resolution allow for smooth, effortless control — just like a tablet device. Cloud Navigation

Cloud Navigation* bring maps, personalization, and the SubaruConnect Intelligent Assistant together in the multimedia system. The system allows you to use voice-activated commands or the touchscreen to search for directions, find points of interest, and explore local businesses. Features of Cloud Navigation include route details, turn-by-turn guidance options, alternate routes, and views of live traffic. The map information is based on your vehicle’s location and sets your routes with the latest traffic and road updates.

Android Auto™

Whether on your phone screen or on your in-vehicle display, Android Auto™ was designed with safety in mind. With larger touch targets, a simplified interface, and easy-to-use voice actions, it’s designed to minimize distraction so you can stay focused on the road.

Apple CarPlay®

Apple CarPlay® is the smarter, safer way to use your iPhone® in your Subaru. CarPlay® allows you to get directions, make calls, send and receive messages, and listen to music while keeping your focus on the road. Just connect your iPhone® and go.

Bluetooth®

Subaru offers standard Bluetooth® hands-free technology on every model to manage phone and audio systems. Bluetooth®-enabled mobile phones link directly through your Subaru audio system automatically, once connected. Receive and initiate calls or play music directly from your device, all while keeping it conveniently stowed away.
