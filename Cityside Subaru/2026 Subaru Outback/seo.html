Inside the 2026 Subaru Outback Boston, MA, drivers receive the standard Subaru 12.1-inch Multimedia System with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™. Comfort stands out in the 2026 Subaru Outback Boston, MA. Power windows, power door locks and a tilt-and-telescoping steering column help customize the cabin quickly. Those who want more refinement can add perforated Nappa leather seats, a 12-way power driver’s seat with memory, an 8-way power front passenger seat, heated rear outboard seats, ventilated front seats, a heated steering wheel and an auto-dimming smart rear-view mirror.
