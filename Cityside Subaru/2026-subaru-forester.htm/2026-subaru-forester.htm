Capability and Efficiency

A fuel-efficient compact SUV with gas mileage of up to 33 MPG*, every Forester features the legendary capability and dependable confidence of standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive. Designed to optimize traction in rain, snow, and on rough roads, this system constantly evaluates acceleration, braking, and cornering in real time to continuously deliver power to all four wheels. If a wheel slips, power is instantaneously transferred to the wheels that have the most grip.

Stability and Control

With its exceptionally balanced layout, Subaru Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive delivers a solid connection with the road and an engaged, responsive drive. The entire system lies along the centerline of the vehicle, balancing weight distribution between the two sides to help provide optimal performance and control.

Durability

With the proven engineering of Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive, which has demonstrated its durability and low maintenance over four decades of owner-tested Subaru vehicles, you can be confident your Forester will keep taking you farther year after year.

Up to 9.3 Inches of Ground Clearance

With 9.3 inches of ground clearance, the Forester Wilderness is equipped to take on ruts, rocks, and other obstacles on the trail, making it the perfect choice for your next off-road SUV. All other Forester models feature 8.7 inches of clearance to traverse deep snow and mud. The Forester features available X-MODE® with Hill Descent Control, which electronically optimizes the engine, transmission, and other systems for increased traction in slippery conditions. Available dual-function X-MODE® gives you extra control over the traction system with settings for snow, dirt, deep snow, and mud.

Every 2026 Forester AWD compact SUV features standard EyeSight® Driver Assist Technology, which acts like an extra set of eyes on the road and if need be, an extra foot on the brake*. Pre-Collision Braking
