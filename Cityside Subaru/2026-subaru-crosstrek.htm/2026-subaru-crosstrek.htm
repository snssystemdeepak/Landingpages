Standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive SUV

Standard Symmetrical
All-Wheel Drive SUV

Capability

Every 2026 Subaru Crosstrek SUV features the capability of standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive. This system is designed to optimize traction in rain, snow, on bad roads, or even when there’s no road at all. It constantly evaluates acceleration, braking, and cornering in real time to continuously deliver power to all four wheels. If a wheel slips, power is instantaneously transferred to the wheels that have the most grip.

Stability and Control

With its exceptionally balanced layout, Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive delivers a solid connection to the road and an engaged, responsive drive. The entire system lies along the centerline of the vehicle, equalizing weight distribution between the two sides to help provide optimal performance and control.

A Durable & Fuel-Efficient SUV

Each 2026 Crosstrek model delivers competitive gas mileage and driving range. With the proven engineering and decades of adventure behind Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive — which has demonstrated its durability and low maintenance over four decades of owner-tested Subaru vehicles — you can be confident your Crosstrek will be with you for all the miles ahead.

Up to 9.3 Inches of Ground Clearance

All Crosstrek models feature standard dual-function X-MODE® with Hill Descent Control — which electronically optimizes the engine, transmission, and other systems for increased traction in slippery conditions with settings for deep snow, dirt, and mud.
