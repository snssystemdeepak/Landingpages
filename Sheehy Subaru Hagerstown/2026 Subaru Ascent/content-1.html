There’s always room for more stories in the 2026 Crosstrek.

Now there are three different takes on the flexible capability of the Crosstrek: Get more fuel-efficiency and lower emissions with the new 2026 Crosstrek Hybrid. Pick the rugged 2026 Crosstrek Wilderness for off-road adventure. The flexible 2026 Crosstrek conventional trims provide miles and miles of reliable exploration. All Crosstrek compact SUV models feature standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive, impressive ground clearance, and a comfortable interior that can fit a surprising amount of passengers or cargo.

MySubaru Navigator

MySubaru Navigator* is a subscription-based service that provides seamless turn-by-turn navigation directly from your vehicle. Get real-time traffic updates, route guidance, and points of interest so you can get where you're going with confidence.

Please note: Navigation features vary by vehicle and trim. Check your vehicle’s specifications or contact your Subaru retailer for details.

AT&T® Wi-Fi Hotspot

Stay connected on the go with built-in Wi-Fi connectivity in select Subaru vehicles. With the 4G LTE Wi-Fi hotspot, passengers can stream and share their favorite content online*. With support for multiple devices, everyone can enjoy streaming TV shows, movies, music, playing games, checking email, and more right from the moment you start your Subaru.

SiriusXM® with 360L

All SiriusXM®-equipped new Subaru vehicles come with a four-month* trial subscription. With the SiriusXM® trial subscription, get access to all your favorite entertainment from SiriusXM® to enjoy in your vehicle. Outside the vehicle, use the SiriusXM® app, which includes ad-free music and talk, sports, comedy, news, podcasts, and more. You can also unlock exclusives on the SiriusXM® app that bring you closer to your favorite stars, artists, creators, and athletes.

TomTom® Navigation

The MySubaru Connected Services touchscreen navigation system’s intuitive interface and high resolution allow for smooth, effortless control — just like a tablet device. There’s also state-of-the-art navigation available from the industry leader TomTom® combining the benefits of an in-dash device with the advanced features of TomTom’s portable navigation devices.

Cloud Navigation

Cloud Navigation* bring maps, personalization, and the SubaruConnect Intelligent Assistant together in the multimedia system. The system allows you to use voice-activated commands or the touchscreen to search for directions, find points of interest, and explore local businesses. Features of Cloud Navigation include route details, turn-by-turn guidance options, alternate routes, and views of live traffic. The map information is based on your vehicle’s location and sets your routes with the latest traffic and road updates.

Android Auto™

Whether on your phone screen or on your in-vehicle display, Android Auto™ was designed with safety in mind. With larger touch targets, a simplified interface, and easy-to-use voice actions, it’s designed to minimize distraction so you can stay focused on the road.

Apple CarPlay®

Apple CarPlay® is the smarter, safer way to use your iPhone® in your Subaru. CarPlay® allows you to get directions, make calls, send and receive messages, and listen to music while keeping your focus on the road. Just connect your iPhone® and go.

Bluetooth®

Subaru offers standard Bluetooth® hands-free technology on every model to manage phone and audio systems. Bluetooth®-enabled mobile phones link directly through your Subaru audio system automatically, once connected. Receive and initiate calls or play music directly from your device, all while keeping it conveniently stowed away.
