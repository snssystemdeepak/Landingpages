Bold Style Meets Premium Design

With its powerful gloss black and chrome front grille; geometric wheel design; and modern, contoured lines set off with stylish accents, the 2026 Ascent is a bold, stylish take on the family SUV.

Power Rear Gate

A standard Power Rear Gate allows automatic opening even when your arms are full, and for added convenience, a single touch of the gate button lets it close and lock the entire vehicle. The opening height can also be adjusted when clearance is limited.
