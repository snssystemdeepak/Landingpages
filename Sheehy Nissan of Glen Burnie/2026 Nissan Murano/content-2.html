Remote Temperature Control

When the weather takes a turn, use the MyNISSAN® app to remotely start your Nissan and preheat or precool it to your desired temperature before you go.*

Wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™

Make your Nissan an extension of your compatible iPhone® with wireless Apple CarPlay® integration and your compatible smartphone with Android Auto™ capability. Use all your favorite apps right from the touch-screen display.*

Wi-Fi Hotspot

NissanConnect® Wi-Fi Hotspot provides you with the ability to connect up to seven compatible devices to keep you more connected on the go. Stream, browse, share, and more with a fast, powerful connection right from the comfort of your vehicle.*

USB Ports

Plug into Nissan’s USB connectivity. Keep your devices charged and ready so you never miss a beat on every journey.*

Additional Driver

NissanConnect® Services shares the love, allowing personalized settings for up to four additional drivers. Just connect, get behind the wheel, and you’ll feel right at home.*

Walk Away Status

Leave your Nissan with the confidence of knowing the MyNISSAN® app can notify you if you’ve left your windows down or your doors unlocked.*

Remote Vehicle Status

The MyNISSAN® app can remotely monitor key vehicle data like tire pressure, lock/unlock status, miles to empty, and more right from your compatible smartphone.*

SiriusXM®

Expand your listening options through SiriusXM® with 360L to enjoy a wide variety of ad-free music, plus sports, news, talk, on-demand content, and curated recommendations right in your vehicle.*

Google™ built-in

Connect to your Nissan like never before with available Google™ built-in. Control Google Maps™, climate, and more with your voice. Download your favorite apps seamlessly via Google Play™.*

Connected Garage Door Opener with myQ®

Use NissanConnect® Services to open and close your compatible garage door and even get notifications if it was left open via myQ®.*
