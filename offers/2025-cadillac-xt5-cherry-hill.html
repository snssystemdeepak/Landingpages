FWD DRIVER MODES

  • TOUR: The most common mode for everyday driving, with tuning and calibration set for a smooth ride.

  • SPORT: Calibrated for an enhanced feeling of road connection and improved steering at higher speeds.

AWD DRIVER MODES

  • TOUR: The most common mode for everyday driving, with tuning and calibration set for a smooth ride.
  • AWD: Engages the front and rear wheels to help improve normal driving on paved roads that are covered with water, snow or ice.
  • SPORT: AWD is engaged, improving vehicle handling and acceleration on dry pavement. Provides an enhanced feeling of road connection and more confident steering at higher speeds for a fun-to-drive experience.
  • OFF-ROAD: Off-Road is designed to engage the front and rear wheels to help improve traction, in off-road conditions such as gravel, sand and mud.
