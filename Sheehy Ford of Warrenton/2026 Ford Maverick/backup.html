Exterior

Stuff You Need To Get Stuff Done

Engineered with the versatility that’s meant to do what trucks were designed to do from the beginning. With a flair of style for the modern truck owner.

Rolling Blackout

The available Black Appearance Package for Maverick® XLT and Lariat® includes a black painted roof and mirrors, 19-inch Black Aluminum wheels, dark-appearance headlamps, and a Black Edition decal on the hood.

Cargo Bed

The cargo bed offers 33.3 cu ft of space inside the sidewalls with the multi-position tailgate fully closed* — enough for 16 standard bags of mulch.

Lift-In Height

For easier loading and unloading, every model features a bed floor no higher than 30.4 inches with the Multi-Position Tailgate lowered. That’s lower than a standard kitchen countertop.

Length

It’s 54.4" from the cab rear wall to the tailgate. You can also add more with the available bed extender accessory. Lock the multi-position tailgate in line with the wheel wells for a flat-loading surface with secure attachment points.

Width

From sidewall to sidewall, the bed measures 53.3" — wider than a typical full-size mattress. With 42.6" of space at the wheel wells, there’s also enough room for a commonly-sized riding lawn mower.

In-Bed Power

The two standard pre-wired 12-volt connections can keep a mini-fridge or other essentials humming. Meanwhile, an available Pro Power Onboard™*, 110-volt outlet lets you charge your devices, power drill, or air pump.

LED Projector Headlamps

See the road ahead clearly with available LED projector headlamps — while LED accent lights help ensure you’ll be seen clearly, too. Comes standard on the Maverick Lobo™, Lariat®, and Tremor® model.

interior

Room for Five. Ready To Roam.

Unique cloth bucket seats delivering stylish and welcoming comfort. Plus, seating for five and ample storage space allow you to bring along your favorite people and their things.

Party of Five

Up to five people can ride in style comfortably. Available ActiveX®-trimmed seats are durable and easy to clean. They repel grime and hold up to the wear and tear of almost any outing.

Soak It All In

The available power moonroof comes fully equipped with sunbeams, brilliant stars, and fresh air.

Under-Seat Storage

The cargo bed can hold a lot. There’s also additional storage under the rear seat. Use it for more space — or to help securely keep your tools and sports gear inside the cab.

Accommodating Door Pockets

Smart use of space means you can find storage throughout the Maverick® pickup. There’s even a custom-designed door pocket, sized just right for most water bottles.

Next Best Thing to a Private Passing Lane

The available Ford Co-Pilot360® Assist 2.0 Package* is designed to help free up stress when you travel. While it can’t do everything, it will help you feel more in command behind the wheel. This package comes available for the Maverick® Lobo™ model and standard on Tremor® and Lariat® models. Here are a selection of the features included in Ford Co-Pilot360 Assist 2.0.

Adaptive Cruise Control

This available feature eases your stress on the road using Adaptive Cruise Control with Stop-and-Go,* Lane Centering, and Predictive Speed Assist. These features help you maintain your preferred speed, slow and stop your vehicle in traffic, and adjust your speed while navigating curves — all while keeping your vehicle centered in its lane.*

BLIS® (Blind Spot Information System with Trailer Coverage)

The available BLIS with Trailer Coverage can help ease lane-change anxiety – even with a trailer in tow. It can alert you not only when a vehicle is spotted in your blind spots but also when one is alongside the trailer.*

Intersection Assist

Available Intersection Assist uses the front camera and radar sensors to detect oncoming traffic while you are attempting to turn left. If there’s a risk of a potential collision with an oncoming vehicle, your vehicle can alert you and apply the brakes. This just might give you more confidence when attempting to make a left turn at an intersection.*

13.2" Center Display

Keep your favorite apps within reach. Maverick® pickup offers a standard 13.2" center display for easy access.

Available 360-Degree Camera

Improve visibility while you’re backing up. Front, side and rear cameras provide visibility around your pickup, help avoiding obstacles and keeping pedestrians in view.*

Performance

THE Maverick® WITH A WILD SIDE

Just press the accelerator. Maverick® Lobo™ pickup responds with spirited performance and nimble cornering. Streamline design and unique ground effects let the wind slip past you as you comb the city.

Power Without the Bulk

The Ford Maverick® pickup is for those who require truck performance but in a downsized package. It's the right-sized pickup equipped with a standard 2.5L hybrid or available 2.0L EcoBoost® engine. Perfect for those who simply want to break out — and if needed — to bring along a little extra.

2.5L Hybrid Engine

Standard on XL, XLT, and Lariat® models, this gas and electric-driven engine delivers the power needed to take on city gridlock. Or when equipped with available AWD — to get as far from it as you can.

2.0L EcoBoost® ENGINE

Available on XL, XLT, and Lariat® models, and standard on Maverick® Lobo™ and Tremor® models, the EcoBoost® gas engine offers 250 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque.* Tow a heavy load on steep grades.* Head off-road* to a remote campsite. Whatever you choose.

Tremor®

Off-road tuned suspension. An electronic-locking rear differential. Trail Control™* with Trail 1-Pedal Drive. An elevated ride height and more. Put together, you’ve got capability to go out and get dirty.*

FX4® Off-Road Package

The FX4® Off-Road Package, available on XLT, will inspire you to dive into the thick of things.* Features include performance suspension, skid plates, and all-terrain tires.

Towing & Hauling

The Unofficial "Help-Friends-Move" Pickup

Just press the accelerator. Maverick® Lobo™ pickup responds with spirited performance and nimble cornering. Streamline design and unique ground effects let the wind slip past you as you comb the city.

Flat-Tow Capability

Maverick® trucks, with the 2.5L hybrid engine, can be towed with all four wheels down.* Just hitch your truck to a properly equipped RV.*

1,500 lbs. Max Payload

Select Maverick® trims are rated to haul up to 1,500 lbs of payload. Bring a couch, a lawnmower, or a dirt bike — and perhaps a few friends, too.*

4K Tow Package

The weekend has arrived. With a tow rating up to 4,000 lbs,* the available 4K Tow Package lets you hitch up and split town. Features include a trailer hitch (Class III) 2" receiver with 4-pin and 7-pin connector, trailer brake controller*, and upgraded cooling fan. On models equipped with an EcoBoost® engine, there’s also a higher capacity radiator.*

Pro Trailer Hitch Assist™

This system uses the rear camera and corner radar to help guide your truck into place for hitching.* Guide lines are shown on the 13.2" center display.

Pro Trailer Backup Assist™

With Pro Trailer Backup Assist™, reversing is as easy as turning a knob.* It only takes one time to enter a few trailer measurements into the system and calibrate the trailer. Then rotate the knob in the direction you want the trailer to go, while the system controls the steering wheel.

Towing Guide

Whether you’re towing a camper, cargo trailer, or something similar, our handy towing guide will help you determine if this truck is capable of towing what you need.*
