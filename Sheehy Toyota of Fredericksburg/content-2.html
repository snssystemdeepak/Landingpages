DESIGN

Built for the bold.

Ruggedly refined, both inside and out. Tacoma is the perfect blend of capability and style.

High-Clearance Front Bumper

TRD Pro’s and Trailhunter’s standard high-clearance front bumper enhances Tacoma’s approach angle—providing even greater confidence when you’re traversing tough terrain.

Exterior Styling

In a nod to its Baja racing heritage, Tacoma continues to embody the extreme spirit of outdoor adventure while staying true to its iconic design.

Premium LED Headlights

Featuring dynamic sequential turn signals and Daytime Running Lights (DRL), Tacoma’s available premium LED headlights blend form, function and safety.

Aggressive Stance

Building upon its iconic design DNA of a high-lift, streamlined body and a powerful, athletic stance, Tacoma sports a look that’s always ready to hit the trail.

Rear Cabin Storage

Tacoma features a range of innovative storage solutions, depending on cab configuration, such as rear seats that flip up or fold down to reveal additional storage compartments, or a fold-flat passenger seat and lockable rear storage panel on XtraCab models.

Interior Styling

Tacoma’s modernized interior is the perfect blend of fun and functionality. With a unique look and feel tailored to each specific grade, plus advanced comfort, technology, safety and convenience features, Tacoma is your ideal partner in adventure.

14-In. Multimedia Touchscreen

Everything is right at your fingertips with Tacoma’s available 14-in. multimedia touchscreen display, which supports satellite navigation, SiriusXM® and wireless Apple CarPlay® or Android Auto™ compatibility.

10-Speaker JBL® Premium Audio

Keep the good vibes going from the trail to the campsite and beyond with the available JBL® Premium Audio system and JBL® FLEX portable Bluetooth® speaker.

 

TRD

The Real Deal

When it comes to off-road heritage, there’s only one name you need to know: Toyota Racing Development (TRD).

TRD Family

The Tacoma TRD Family doesn’t just bring your toys to the sandbox; they’re what you bring to play in the sandbox. From the capable TRD Off-Road to the badass TRD Pro, there’s a TRD for whatever sandbox you play in.

IsoDynamic Performance Front Seats

Designed to help keep you stable over unstable terrain, TRD Pro’s segment-first IsoDynamic Performance front seats use an air-over-oil shock absorber system to dampen body movement and stabilize your field of vision—helping provide a smooth, confidence-inspiring ride. So go ahead and let it rip.

TRD-Tuned FOX® Suspension

Experience superior performance with TRD-tuned FOX®* QS3®* Internal Bypass shocks, with 2.5-in. aluminum housings and rear piggyback reservoirs. These cutting-edge shocks ensure fade-free, high-speed performance, and with QS3®* technology, you can adjust the level of damping to match whatever terrain you’re tackling.

33-In. Rugged-Terrain Tires and 18-In. Wheels

Formed to function. TRD Pro’s unique 18-in. black alloy wheels are constructed with flow-formed aluminum alloy to provide an ideal balance of strength and light weight. Plus, the standard 33-in. Goodyear®* Territory® tires feature a wraparound shoulder design for added off-road traction, and a sequenced pitch pattern for reduced on-road noise.

 

TRD Off-Road Suspension

Tacoma TRD Off-Road comes equipped with the most extreme suspension setup in its history. Co-engineered with Bilstein®,* the monotube shock absorbers feature rear piggyback reservoirs for extended travel and efficient heat dissipation, as well as End Stop Control Valve (ESCV) technology.

TRAILHUNTER

From off-road to off-grid.

Toyota has long been the choice of those who forge their own paths to adventure. Now, the choice is even clearer. Conceived, designed and engineered to take on the toughest trails, Tacoma Trailhunter can also carry your gear and supplies for extended time off the grid. It is a ready-made overlanding beast.

Old Man Emu® Suspensio

Trailhunter’s standard Old Man Emu®* 2.5-in. forged monotube position-sensitive shocks with rear piggyback reservoirs combine rugged capability with outstanding load-carrying stability, to get you through the obstacles and into the middle of nowhere.

33-In. Rugged-Terrain Tires and 18-In. Wheels

As the old saying goes, “Go big or go home.” So of course, Trailhunter goes big, with 33-in. Goodyear®* Territory® R/T tires wrapped around 18-in. bronze-finished wheels. These rugged-terrain tires boost ground clearance while delivering superior off-road traction. Where you’re going, you’ll need it.

Onboard Air Compressor

Whether you're spending days or even weeks on the trail, Trailhunter’s bed-mounted air compressor makes airing the tires either up or down a breeze. Simply set the desired pressure level and let Trailhunter do the rest.

ARB® Steel Rear Bumper

Co-developed with ARB®, Trailhunter’s standard high-clearance steel rear bumper provides additional protection for when the going gets tough. Plus, with integrated front and rear recovery hooks mounted to the frame, you’ll have peace of mind that you won’t be stranded when the going gets tough.

High-Clearance Trail Exhaust

Trailhunter’s standard high-clearance trail exhaust exits before the rear axle, providing enhanced ground clearance and departure angles—allowing you to take on tougher terrain than you thought possible.

Rigid Industries® Fog Lights and Integrated Scene Lighting

Conditions won’t always be ideal, but that’s half the fun anyway. Trailhunter features Rigid Industries®* LED fog lights that can switch between amber and white light to suit the conditions on the trail ahead. When you get to camp, additional LED lighting integrated inside the bed and on the bed cap can illuminate your gear as well as provide ambient light outside the vehicle.

ARB® Sport Bar

Designed for both utility and style, Trailhunter’s standard modular sport bar was co-engineered with ARB® and provides a sturdy anchor point for securing cargo. The integrated MOLLE panels can be removed to effortlessly adapt to your specific needs or upgraded to the available full bed rack.
