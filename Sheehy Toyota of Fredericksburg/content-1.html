PERFORMANCE & CAPABILITY

Embrace every obstacle.

Tacoma is built to take on whatever’s in front of you. With a range of purpose-built suspension options, and available trail-conquering features like a multi-link rear suspension, Stabilizer Disconnect Mechanism (SDM) and Crawl Control (CRAWL), it never met a challenge it didn’t like.

i-FORCE MAX Hybrid Powertrain

Tacoma’s available i-FORCE MAX hybrid powertrain introduces a prodigious level of torque to your world. The 2.4-liter turbocharged engine, combined with a 48-HP electric motor, delivers up to a commanding 326 maximum horsepower and an astounding 465 lb.-ft. of low-end torque.

i-FORCE Gas Engine

Meet every challenge, every day. With up to 278 horsepower and up to 317 lb.-ft. of ultra-responsive torque, Tacoma’s i-FORCE 2.4-liter turbocharged engine is ready to deliver day in, day out. And when adventure calls, enjoy the confidence of knowing your Tacoma is built to get you there and back.

6-Speed Manual Transmission

Tacoma puts control of the i-FORCE 2.4-liter turbocharged engine in the palm of your hands. With Tacoma’s available 6-speed intelligent Manual Transmission (iMT2) with automatic rev-matching and anti-stall technology, those challenging rock-crawling sections become a little less challenging.

Multi-Link Rear Suspension With Coil Springs

Whatever the journey ahead, Tacoma’s available multi-link rear suspension with coil springs keeps you in command by enhancing traction and delivering maximum suspension articulation. Whether you’re traversing rocky landscapes or pounding the pavement, you can unleash the full potential of your Tacoma with confidence.

 

Electronically Locking Rear Differential

When you’re traversing uneven terrain, Tacoma’s available electronically controlled locking rear differential provides increased grip in low-traction conditions. By distributing engine power evenly, it allows the rear wheels to move at the same speed, pushing you up, over and through the obstacle.

Stabilizer Disconnect Mechanism

Unleash Tacoma’s full off-road capability and get farther down the trail with the available Stabilizer Disconnect Mechanism (SDM). With the push of a button, you can increase suspension articulation across the whole truck to dominate the off-road like never before.

Multi-Terrain Select and Crawl Control

Operable in both 4WD high and low ranges, Tacoma’s available Multi-Terrain Select helps maintain control on challenging trails by adjusting engine throttle and traction control to suit the conditions, while Tacoma’s available Crawl Control (CRAWL)* system acts as a low-speed, off-road cruise control, automatically modulating the throttle and brakes in one of five selectable speeds.

Underbody Protection

Sometimes the trail bites back, so all Tacoma trucks come standard with underbody protection. Trailhunter takes it one step further, with frame-mounted rock rails as well as enhanced skid plates protecting the engine, transfer case, rear differential and fuel tank.

 

TECHNOLOGY & UTILITY

Built with brains and brawn.

Advanced Towing Technology

Tacoma allows you to tow with confidence, thanks to available advanced technology like Trailer Backup Guide with Straight Path Assist, an integrated brake controller, and Blind Spot Monitor (BSM) that extends the area of detection when it identifies a trailer is being towed.

12.3-In. Digital Gauge Cluster

Quickly see all the information you need with Tacoma’s available 12.3-in. digital gauge cluster with selectable gauges. This customizable full-color display allows access to safety features, navigation, audio controls, off-road features, towing functions and more.

14-In. Audio Multimedia Display

Tacoma’s impressive available 14-in. multimedia touchscreen display runs on Toyota’s latest audio multimedia system, which supports satellite navigation,* SiriusXM®,* wireless Apple CarPlay® compatibility* and more.

3D Multi-Terrain Monitor

3D Multi-Terrain Monitor

To help you get a better view of your immediate surroundings and even the ground below, Tacoma's available next-generation Multi-Terrain Monitor (MTM) lets you toggle between front-, rear- and side-camera views to check for potential obstacles.

Bed Utility

Available in either 5-ft. or 6-ft. configurations, Tacoma’s aluminum-reinforced bed is composed of an ultra-durable Sheet-Molded Composite (SMC) surface that is strong, resilient and lightweight. There’s also a slew of useful available features, like an onboard air compressor, a 2400W AC power supply,* exterior LED scene lighting, and more.

2400-Watt Power Supply

Keep your gear running and the good times rolling, wherever you are. All hybrid Tacoma trucks come standard with a 2400W onboard AC inverter* that sends power to both the bed and inside the cabin.

TNGA-F Chassis

Adopting the same advanced and robust chassis of the full-size Tundra and Sequoia, Tacoma trucks are placed on the Toyota New Global Architecture-F (TNGA-F) platform that combines high-strength steel with lightweight aluminum and laser welds, for enhanced rigidity and less weight throughout the chassis.

Prewired Auxiliary Switches

Tacoma TRD Pro and Trailhunter models offer a fourth prewired auxiliary toggle switch integrated into the dashboard to provide the perfect platform to install Genuine Toyota Accessories and other approved accessory products.*

Safety & Convenience

Power Open-and-Close Tailgate

Tacoma’s available segment-first power open-and-close tailgate with jam protection makes loading and unloading your gear a breeze. If your hands are full, just press a button on the key fob or push the taillight switch to open and close, or simply let the knee-lift assist close the tailgate for you.

Power-Extending Running Boards

Step into your comfort zone. Standard power running boards on Limited grade are concealed when the door is closed, then extend outward when the door is opened to allow for easier entry and exit of your Tacoma, as well as access to hard-to-reach cargo in the bed.

Blind Spot Monitor With Rear Cross-Traffic Alert

Tacoma's available Blind Spot Monitor (BSM) is designed to warn you when a vehicle in the next lane enters your blind spot on either side of the vehicle. And when you're reversing out of a tight spot, available Rear Cross-Traffic Alert (RCTA) helps detect vehicles approaching from either side.

Digital Rearview Mirror

Connected to a camera in the rear, Tacoma’s available digital rearview mirror with integrated HomeLink®* universal transceiver buttons casts a clear, wide and obstruction-free view of what’s behind you. You can zoom in, zoom out and adjust the mirror to give you a better perspective.

No Cost Maintenance Plan

Every new Toyota comes with ToyotaCare,* a maintenance plan that covers normal factory scheduled service for 2 years or 25,000 miles, whichever comes first, and 24-hour Roadside Assistance for 2 years, unlimited mileage.

Toyota Safety Sense™ 3.0*

Pre-Collision System With Pedestrian Detection

PCS w/PD* can alert you to potential collisions and may automatically brake.

Full-Speed Range Dynamic Radar Cruise Control

DRCC* helps maintain a preset distance from the vehicle ahead of you, from highway speeds down to a full stop.

Lane Departure Alert With Steering Assist

LDA w/SA* may alert you to inadvertent lane departures and make minor steering corrections to help keep you in your lane.

Lane Tracing Assist

LTA* helps you center the vehicle within its lane while DRCC* is active.

Road Sign Assist

RSA* can recognize certain road signs and display them to you inside the vehicle.

Automatic High Beams

AHB* can automatically toggle between high and low beams based on the vehicle’s surroundings.

Proactive Driving Assist

PDA* may provide gentle braking and/or steering to help control distance between your vehicle and a vehicle, pedestrian or bicyclist ahead of you.

Keep the good vibes going from the trail to the campsite and beyond with the available 10-speaker JBL®* Premium Audio system. The available integrated JBL® FLEX* portable, self-powered Bluetooth® speaker is waterproof and dust-proof, so it can be carted just about anywhere.
