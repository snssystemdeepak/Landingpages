Get to Know the 2026 Toyota Tacoma Richmond, VA, from Sheehy Toyota

The 2026 Toyota Tacoma Richmond, VA, available at Sheehy Toyota, brings a versatile approach to midsize truck design. With a long list of trims that range from the SR to TRD Pro, this Toyota truck delivers strength for towing, capability for weekend trails and comfort for daily routes across the Richmond area.

Which Toyota dealers have the 2026 Toyota Tacoma near Richmond, VA, in stock?

We do! Find the comfort you crave in a Toyota truck built for the long haul. Inside the 2026 Toyota Tacoma Richmond, VA, drivers find a cab built for utility and long-term comfort. Cloth seats come standard along with 6-way manually adjustable front seats, a tilt-and-telescopic steering wheel and fold-flat passenger seating that opens space for gear. Optional SofTex® seats, heated and ventilated front seats, 8-way driver power adjustability and 4-way front passenger power adjustability elevate the driving experience. Available dual zone automatic climate control, a heated steering wheel and a digital rearview mirror with HomeLink® expand comfort further.

Performance in the 2026 Toyota Tacoma Richmond, VA, begins with a standard i-FORCE 2.4-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine that creates 228 horsepower and 243 lb-ft of torque. Configurations equipped with a 6-foot bed use an 8-speed automatic transmission, while 5-foot bed models feature a 6-speed manual transmission. Rear-wheel drive is standard. For more strength, an upgraded version of the same engine produces 278 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque, along with available 4-wheel drive. Drivers wanting maximum output can choose the i-FORCE MAX 2.4-liter turbocharged inline-4 hybrid powertrain that delivers 326 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque, giving the 2026 Toyota Tacoma Richmond, VA, strong acceleration and confident performance for demanding jobs.

The 2026 Toyota Tacoma Richmond, VA, offers functional exterior features built around flexibility. Standard equipment includes 17-inch steel wheels, LED headlights, LED bed lighting, a choice of 5-foot or 6-foot beds and a deck rail system with four adjustable tie-down cleats and fixed cargo bed tie-down points. Black door handles, variable intermittent wipers and black side window trim add straightforward utility. Optional upgrades include 18-inch Limited alloy wheels, a composite front skid plate with underbody transfer case and fuel tank protection, a chrome-finished exhaust tip and Rigid Industries® white-and-amber-color switching LED fog lights. A power sliding rear window with privacy glass, power running boards and a power tilt-and-slide moonroof bring more convenience.

Standard technology in the 2026 Toyota Tacoma Richmond, VA, includes an 8-inch touchscreen, Bluetooth®, USB ports, push button start and wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™. A Smart Key System and a 4-speaker audio system come standard as well. Optional upgrades bring a major tech boost with a 14-inch touchscreen, a 10-speaker JBL® audio system, a 12.3-inch digital gauge cluster, a Head-Up Display, navigation, a wireless charger and Remote Connect.

Toyota Safety Sense™ 3.0 comes standard on the 2026 Toyota Tacoma Richmond, VA. Features include a Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection, a Proactive Driving Assist, Lane Departure Alerts with Steering Assist, Automatic High Beams, Road Sign Assist and Full-Speed Range Dynamic Radar Cruise Control. Optional systems expand visibility and convenience with a 3D Panoramic View Monitor, Blind Spot Monitor with Rear Cross-Traffic Alerts and Front and Rear Parking Assist with Automatic Braking and Pedestrian Detection.
