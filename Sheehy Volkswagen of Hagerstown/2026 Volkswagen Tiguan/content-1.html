SAFETY

A lot goes in before you do

Safety is a core value to us. And while we can’t predict everything you might encounter, we can and do spend long hours trying to help you prepare for it.

Rear View Camera System.
Stay focused.

Benefit from the view of a wide lens while you’re reversing. It can help you see obstacles when backing up. It's a little extra help where you want it.

Safety Cell
We’ve got a firm attitude about safety.

Front and rear crumple zones help absorb crash energy, while a rigid safety cage helps distribute forces away from the driver and passengers.

Six stability-enhancing systems

Equipped for peace of mind.

From Electronic Brake-pressure Distribution (EBD), which helps maintain appropriate stopping power during a hard-braking situation, to Electronic Stability Control (ESC), which can adjust engine throttle and apply corrective forces to the wheels that need them most, your vehicle is equipped with a total of six stability-enhancing systems.

Six stability-enhancing systems

Equipped for peace of mind.

From Electronic Brake-pressure Distribution (EBD), which helps maintain appropriate stopping power during a hard-braking situation, to Electronic Stability Control (ESC), which can adjust engine throttle and apply corrective forces to the wheels that need them most, your vehicle is equipped with a total of six stability-enhancing systems.

Intelligent Crash Response System (ICRS)*
Intelligent by design.

In the event of a collision that deploys the airbags*, the ICRS can, in certain circumstances, turn off the fuel pump, unlock the doors, and activate the hazard lights.* It helps react if you might not be able to.

Automatic Post-Collision Braking System
We like to think ahead.

Not every collision brings a car to stop. That’s why our vehicles include the Automatic Post-Collision Braking System. It can also help slow the vehicle down after a collision, helping reduce the chance of any additional impacts.*
